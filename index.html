Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You will win the game by matching your total score to the target score. You will lose the game if you total score goes above the random number. So be careful!

The value of each crystal will remain hidden. Find out each crystals value by clicking on it.

Remember, Each time a new round starts the values for each crystal will change.

Good Luck!
